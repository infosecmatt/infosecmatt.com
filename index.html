Howdy, I'm Matt Johnson.

Professional Summary: After graduating college, I joined Weaver as a cybersecurity consultant and auditor after having done an internship with them the summer before. My work mostly consists of helping clients with security compliance audits, IT governance frameworks, BCP/DR, vulnerability assessments, and (when I'm lucky) penetration testing. Some of my more memorable engagements so far include a ransomware readiness assessment for a utility provider, a PCI audit for a large cloud provider, a NIST CSF assessment for a financial institution, and an external penetration test for a small bank. My certifications include a Security+ and CCSK, and I plan on becoming an OSCP by next year.

College Summary: In December 2019 I graduated summa cum laude from Texas A&M University with a degree in Management Information Systems. Some of my favorite college memories include studying abroad in Jönköping, Sweden during the fall 2018 semester, working at the Texas A&M Study Abroad Office, and watching the Aggies pull off an amazing OT win to stay undefeated. We proceeded to finish the season 8-5, so that didn't exactly work out.
