Howdy, I’m Matt. I'm a native Texan, a security enthusiast, and a musician. I recently graduated from Texas A&M University with a degree in Management Information Systems.

I had a pretty amazing college experience. I came in as a finance major, thought it was boring. I decided to switch to MIS, which I thought was much cooler. I got an on campus job, and ended up working with the incredible people at Texas A&M's Study Abroad Programs Office for a year and a half. I studied abroad in Jönköping, Sweden for a semester in the fall of 2018 and had a life changing experience. In all, I made lots of great memories with friends and classmates and learned a lot in the process.

As for security, it was during my IT advisory internship at Weaver that I first gained interest and experience in the field. In the time since, I've focused my efforts on honing my networking, penetration testing, and server configuration skills. I'm currently working on my Security+ certificate and hope to get my OSCP within the next two years.

Beyond security, my interests include music, lifting weights, and history. I speak both English and Portuguese, and am currently trying to learn German. If you're interested in getting to know me better, feel free to reach out! I'm always happy to talk.
